What Do These Tradespeople Do? Choose the Correct Job Title for Each Speaker

Read the descriptions and choose the correct profession for each speaker.

A. My job is important. I work with wires. They conduct electricity to all lamps in the house. You cannot have cool air in the summer, see at night, or keep food for long without my efforts.

B. I install things that supply water, oxygen, and different gases in homes, hospitals, etc. The pipes I install are sometimes very small and very big at other times. I can read blueprints and work with engineers.

C. I work both on land and underwater. My job is important and dangerous. You cannot get your water or gas or bring pieces together without my service.

D. Everyone on a worksite should be careful when they see me coming. I make sure everything is in its proper place. Everyone must be wearing their Personal Protective Equipment. When I see a worker, I ask for his work permit, first.
