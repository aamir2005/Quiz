Fire Safety Quiz

1. In case of a fire, you will hear ____________.

a) a loud fire alarm bell
b) a safety officer shouting “Fire! Fire!”
c) a beeping sound on your phone from the Fire Safety App.

2) When you hear the alarm bell, you should stop what you are doing and ___________________.

a) run to the nearest fire exit
b) walk to the nearest fire exit
c) walk or run to the nearest fire exit

3) If you remember that you left your phone in the building, you should ____________________________.

a) walk back inside slowly and get it.
b) ask a manager to go back inside and get your phone.
c) never go back to your workplace to get your phone.

4) IF you are on the second floor, when the fire alarm bells rings, you should __________________________________.

a) use the stairs or the lifts to get out of the building
b) use only the stairs
c) not use the lifts, because you could get stuck if the electricity goes off.

5) You should exit using __________________________________.

a) the nearest door
b) any window or door
c) a fire exit only.

6) Next, you must __________________________________.

a) go to your assembly point outside in the car park.
b) go to your car and sit inside
c) go to your car and drive home.

7) Finally, you must __________________________________.

a) stay at the assembly point until it’s your break time.
b) stay at the assembly point and do whatever the safety officer tells you to do.
c) stay at the assembly point for ten minutes.
