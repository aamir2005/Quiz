English Grammar Quiz

1. Choose the right answer:

The engineers assemble ___________.

2. Question form:

What _______ the engineers assemble?

3. Negative form:

The engineers ____ assemble a map.

4. Choose the right answer:

An electrician fixes ___________.

5. Question form:

What ____ an electrician fix?

6. Negative form:

An electrician______ fix the fridge.

7. Choose the right answer:

A punctual workman arrives at work ___________.

8. Question form:

When ______ a punctual workman arrive at work?

9. Negative form:

A punctual workman _______ arrive late.
